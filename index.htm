Triad Triathlon Team

Our mission is to promote friendship and fitness through multisport activities and to cultivate a high performance athletic community.

The Triad Triathlon Team offers group swims, bike training and runs, monthly meetings, local and national discounts, volunteer opportunities and much, much more...

Maintaing a healthy lifestyle, motivation and support are the real benefits of the organization.

We welcome athletes of all ages and abilities.

Triathlon Season is Coming

Why Join?

Whether you are looking to train for your first or your 100th triathlon, the Triad Triathlon Team has something for you! We offer our opportunities to learn, train and meet like minded individuals in the community. Regular team training sessions will help hone your skills. Team meetings with guest speakers incluing elite athletes and fitness experts will provide valuable insight into the finer points of triathlon.

Join our Facebook Group

Join our Facebook group page to stay up to the minute with all things related to the Triad Triathlon Team. You do not need to be a TTT member to be a part of our Facebook community. We use Facebook to communicate team events, special announcements, upcoming races and much more.
